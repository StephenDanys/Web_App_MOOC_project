About this Course

A distributed system, also known as distributed computing, is a system with multiple components located on different machines that communicate and coordinate actions in order to appear as a single coherent system to the end-user. These courses will serve as an introduction in distributed computing, and in the creation of such a system. You will also learn to use java.net.
